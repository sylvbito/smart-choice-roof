Your tiled roof is built to last decades, but Melbourne's harsh sun, storms, and winter weather take their toll. Our tile roof restoration process brings it back to life.

**Our process:**

1. **Inspection & assessment** — We identify cracked, broken, or slipped tiles and check the underlying sarking and battens.
2. **Pressure cleaning** — High-pressure washing removes moss, lichen, dirt, and old coatings.
3. **Repairs & repointing** — We replace damaged tiles, re-bed ridge capping, and repoint with flexible modern mortar.
4. **Sealing & coating** — Application of premium elastomeric roof coating. UV-resistant, waterproof, and available in Colorbond-matched colours.
5. **Final inspection** — Gutters cleared, debris removed, full walk-around.

All work is fully insured and comes with our 10-year workmanship guarantee.
