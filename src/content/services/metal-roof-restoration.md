Metal roofs are durable, light, and energy-efficient. But they're not immune to rust, fading, or fastener failure. Our metal roof restoration service covers it all.

**Our process:**

1. **Inspection** — We check for rust spots, loose screws, popped fasteners, and damaged flashings.
2. **Preparation** — Surface rust is sanded or chemically treated. Loose fasteners replaced.
3. **Priming** — Anti-corrosion primer on all bare metal areas.
4. **Repainting** — Full application of UV-stable roof paint in your choice of Colorbond colours.
5. **Sealing** — All laps, flashings, and penetrations resealed.

Fully insured with a 7-year workmanship warranty.
